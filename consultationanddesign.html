Consultations & Design

At Piedmont Environmental, our landscape designs are deeply rooted in ecological principles. We believe a beautiful yard should also be a thriving ecosystem — balanced, resilient, and inspiring.

What We Emphasize

  • Designing natural plant communities adapted to the Piedmont region
  • Encouraging biodiversity to reduce pest and maintenance issues
  • Planning for plant succession and ultimate growth size
  • Building healthy, organic-rich soil with balanced pH
  • Creating spaces that support birds, butterflies, and pollinators

What We Can Design

Our work spans from foundation plantings to shade gardens, Piedmont meadows, rain gardens, and more — always integrating natural function with aesthetic beauty.

What to Expect in a Consultation

Each consultation lasts about 90 minutes. We’ll explore your property together, observing the soil, light conditions, existing vegetation, water flow, and how people move through your space. From there, we develop ideas to transform your landscape with ecological intention.
